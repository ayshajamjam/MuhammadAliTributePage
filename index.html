Muhammad Ali

"Float like a butterfly, sting like a bee"

American born but recognized all over the world, Muhammad Ali is considered one of the greatest professional boxers of all time. He fought both in and out of the ring, having actively participated in the civil rights movement and refusing to be drafted to fight in the Vietnam War.

Interesting Facts:

  • At only 18 years old, Ali won a gold medal at the 1960 Summer Olympics in Rome
  • Ali converted to Islam in 1961
  • Ali has nine childen
